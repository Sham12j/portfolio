About Me

I’m a Graphic designer, which basically means I spend my days turning 'Make it pop' into actual design magic. I translate vague client requests like ‘Can you make it more... vibe-y?’ into stunning visuals that speak louder than words. When I’m not battling with layers and color palettes, I experiment with creativity in other forms—like making Pepper Chicken and editing videos (because even my food needs good presentation). Design is my language, coffee is my fuel, and yes, I do judge fonts in the wild!
